Machine Learning Strategy

Our team of quants uses a machine learning model that provides investors with two main features: Stock Price Predictions and a Portfolio Optimizer.

Stock Price Predictions

We feed our stock prediction model with at least 25 years of data, allowing our machine learning algorithm to learn the price movements of stocks in the S&P 500 Index. After learning from the dataset, our algorithm creates 8 different regression models and evaluates the accuracy of each model in predicting the future price levels of stocks.

Portfolio Optimizer

Another feature of our machine learning algorithm is to provide investors with the best portfolio allocation, based on the stocks YOU desire or are interested in. Investors have the option to choose between forming the portfolio with the best Sharpe ratio (best returns per risk) or minimal risk.
